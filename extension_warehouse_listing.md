# Extension Warehouse Listing — Black Magician

## Title
Black Magician — Material Library

## Short Description (140 chars)
Browse finishing materials from vendors, preview with grout & stagger options, and apply directly as SketchUp materials.

## Full Description

Black Magician is a cloud-based finishing material library for interior designers and architects. Browse thousands of tiles, stones, flooring, wood, wallpaper, and wall panels from real vendors — and apply them to your SketchUp model with one click.

### Features

**Material Browsing**
- Browse materials by category: Tile, Stone, Flooring, Wood, Wallpaper, Wall Panel
- Filter by price range, stock availability, and origin
- Search across all vendors instantly
- Save favorites for quick access

**Live Preview**
- Real-time canvas preview before applying
- Grout simulation with adjustable thickness and color
- 1/2 and 1/3 stagger (brick) patterns
- 3×3 random mix mode
- Rotation (0°/90°/180°/270°)
- Hue, saturation, and brightness adjustments

**One-Click Apply**
- Apply directly as a SketchUp material with correct physical dimensions
- Automatically activates Paint Bucket tool
- Supports all tile sizes with accurate mm scaling

**Vendor Portal**
- Vendors upload and manage their own product catalogs
- Real-time stock and pricing information

### Requirements
- SketchUp 2017 or later
- Internet connection (materials are loaded from cloud)

### Pricing
- **Free**: 3 applies per day
- **Pro**: Unlimited applies — ₩3,900/month ($2.99/month)
- 3-day free Pro trial for new users
